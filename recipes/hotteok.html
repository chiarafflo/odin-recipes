Hotteok Recipe

Description

Hotteok is a very popular street food in Korea. It is a fried flat bread with a sweet or savory filling.

You can change the filling to whatever you like! I've done nutella and chocolate chip morsels.

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon of dry yeast
  • 2 teaspoons white sugar
